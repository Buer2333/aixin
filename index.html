Frequently Asked Questions

How much deposit is required? +

A $550 party minimum applies. Exact deposit policy varies by party size — contact us for details.

What if it rains on my event day? +

Please provide a tent, patio, or covered area — our chefs can cook under any covering. Weather-related cancellations must be made 48 hours in advance.

What do I need to prepare? +

Tables, chairs, plates, utensils, salad bowls, and drinks. We bring the chef, grill, and all food.

Do you charge a travel fee? +

Yes — $1 per mile.

How do I pay? +

Cash or PayPal — no extra fee. Venmo and Zelle add a 4% processing fee. Suggested gratuity is 20%.

Can I modify my order after booking? +

Yes. Final food order and headcount can be confirmed the week of your event via text.

Do you handle food allergies? +

Absolutely. Please fill out our Allergy Form so our chefs can prepare safely.

Cancellation & Weather Policy

  • 48 hours notice required for cancellations and rescheduling.
  • If it rains, please provide a tent or covering so our chefs can cook and stay dry.
  • We can cook under tents and patios.
  • You are responsible for canceling due to inclement weather within 48 hours of your party.
